Our aesthetic philosophy

Cosmetics That Work
With Your Biology

Most aesthetic practices operate on a simple premise: you come in, you pick a treatment, you leave. Results are measured in weeks and repeat visits are scheduled accordingly.

We operate differently. Our clinical background in dermatology means we understand skin at a structural and physiological level — not just what a treatment does on the surface, but why skin responds the way it does and how to get it to respond better.

Patients who have done functional medicine work alongside cosmetic treatments consistently see superior outcomes. When inflammation is lower, when hormones are balanced, when the gut-skin axis is functioning correctly — skin texture improves faster, collagen builds more robustly, and treatments hold longer. Internal optimization is the multiplier.

You don't need to be a functional medicine patient to receive cosmetic care here. But if you are, we'll use that knowledge in everything we do.

What sets us apart

Dermatology Expertise. Functional Medicine Context.

A provider with dermatology training approaches a cosmetic treatment differently than an aesthetician or general injector. We understand the anatomy at a level that informs every placement decision, every peel selection, every treatment interval.

And because we also understand what's happening systemically — whether your skin is inflamed at the cellular level, whether your hormones are driving sebum production, whether your thyroid is affecting collagen synthesis — we can sequence treatments intelligently and set accurate expectations based on your actual biology.

This is a combination that's rare in clinical aesthetics. We think it makes a meaningful difference in what you experience and the results you achieve.

What we offer

Treatment Menu

All treatments are performed by or under the supervision of a board-certified PA-C with dermatology training. Pricing and availability discussed at consultation.

Category 01

Skin Quality & Rejuvenation

Medical-Grade Chemical Peels

Skin Renewal

Physician-formulated chemical exfoliation addressing texture, tone, pigmentation, fine lines, and active acne. Peel depth and chemistry are selected based on your skin type, Fitzpatrick classification, and goals — not a one-size menu.

Best for Uneven skin tone, dullness, sun damage, mild acne, texture irregularities, melasma

Microneedling

Collagen Induction

Controlled micro-injury to stimulate collagen and elastin remodeling. Precision depth settings address scarring, pore size, texture, fine lines, and overall skin quality. Can be combined with PRP/PRF, hyaluronic acid, or growth factors.

Best for Acne scarring, enlarged pores, fine lines, lax skin, overall skin quality improvement

PRP / PRF Therapy

Regenerative

Platelet-rich plasma and platelet-rich fibrin concentrate your body's own growth factors to accelerate healing, stimulate collagen, and promote regeneration. Used topically with microneedling or injected for hair restoration and skin rejuvenation.

Best for Hair loss (scalp PRP), deep skin rejuvenation, scar remodeling, skin laxity

Medical-Grade Facials

Maintenance

Clinical-level facial treatments using medical-grade products and protocols — not a spa facial. Designed for active acne management, barrier repair, hydration optimization, and maintenance between more intensive treatments.

Best for Ongoing skin health maintenance, active acne, compromised barrier, sensitive or reactive skin

LED Light Therapy

Non-Invasive

Targeted wavelength light therapy stimulating cellular repair, reducing inflammation, and killing acne-causing bacteria without heat or downtime. Red light supports collagen synthesis; blue light targets P. acnes; near-infrared supports deeper tissue healing.

Best for Active acne, post-procedure recovery, anti-aging support, rosacea calming, sensitized skin

Exosome Therapy

Advanced Regenerative

Next-generation regenerative treatment using cell-signaling molecules to accelerate skin repair, reduce inflammation, and stimulate collagen production — delivering results that go beyond what traditional growth factors achieve alone.

Best for Post-procedure healing, advanced skin rejuvenation, inflammation-driven skin conditions, hair restoration

Category 02

Injectables & Structural Refinement

Neuromodulators

Botox · Dysport · Xeomin

Precise muscle relaxation for dynamic lines — forehead, glabellar (11s), crow's feet, brow lift, lip flip, masseter slimming, hyperhidrosis, and more. Our philosophy is natural movement preservation, not frozen expression. Less is often more.

Best for Dynamic wrinkles, preventive treatment (mid-20s onward), jaw clenching, TMJ, excessive sweating

Dermal Fillers

Hyaluronic Acid · Structural Volume

Hyaluronic acid filler for volume restoration, structural support, and contour refinement — lips, cheeks, jawline, chin, temples, tear troughs, and nasolabial folds. Placed conservatively with anatomical precision. Reversible with hyaluronidase.

Best for Volume loss, lip enhancement, facial contour refinement, hollowing under eyes, asymmetry correction

Collagen Stimulators

Sculptra · Radiesse

Biostimulatory injectables that trigger your body's own collagen production — providing gradual, natural-looking volume restoration that develops over weeks and lasts significantly longer than HA fillers. Ideal for facial rejuvenation and skin quality improvement.

Best for Significant volume loss, facial rejuvenation, hand rejuvenation, patients seeking longer-lasting natural results

Kybella

Fat Dissolution

FDA-approved deoxycholic acid injection that permanently destroys submental fat cells beneath the chin — eliminating the "double chin" without surgery. Results develop gradually over multiple sessions as the body processes treated fat.

Best for Submental fullness (double chin), patients seeking a non-surgical alternative to liposuction

Scalp PRP for Hair Loss

Hair Restoration

Platelet-rich plasma injected directly into the scalp to stimulate dormant follicles, increase hair density, and slow progressive hair loss. Most effective when combined with internal root-cause work addressing the underlying hormonal or nutritional drivers.

Best for Androgenic alopecia, telogen effluvium, thinning in women, early hair loss — best paired with functional medicine evaluation

Combination Protocols

Curated Treatment Plans

The most transformative outcomes come from intelligently sequenced combination approaches — layering regenerative, resurfacing, and injectable treatments across a planned timeline. We build individualized protocols, not one-off appointments.

Best for Patients ready to commit to comprehensive skin transformation with a structured, progressive approach

The internal advantage

Why Internal Health
Amplifies Every Treatment

Cosmetic treatments work with your biology. When that biology is optimized — inflammation controlled, hormones balanced, gut functioning correctly — the outcomes are measurably better.

◎

Faster Healing

Lower systemic inflammation means faster post-treatment recovery, less redness, and quicker return to results after microneedling, peels, and injectables.

◎

Better Collagen Response

Collagen synthesis requires adequate vitamin C, zinc, copper, and amino acids. Nutritional deficiencies — often identified through functional testing — directly limit how well your skin responds to collagen-stimulating treatments.

◎

Longer-Lasting Results

Hormonal imbalances accelerate collagen degradation. Gut-driven inflammation promotes skin breakdown. Addressing these internally extends how long every treatment holds.

◎

Fewer Complications

Immune dysregulation, active gut inflammation, and hormonal volatility can increase adverse responses to aesthetic treatments. Stabilizing physiology internally reduces risk and improves predictability.

Skin Quality Foundations

The internal work that makes every treatment perform better and last longer — whether you're a functional medicine patient or not.

Collagen Nutritional Support

Vitamin C, zinc, copper, and amino acid adequacy — the raw materials for collagen production that no injectable can substitute for.

Inflammation Control

Systemic inflammation degrades collagen, worsens pigmentation, and slows wound healing. Lowering inflammatory burden amplifies every treatment performed.

Hormonal Stability

Estrogen supports collagen synthesis; androgens drive sebum and acne; cortisol degrades skin integrity. Hormone balance is skin care.

UV Protection Protocol

Broad-spectrum SPF 50+ daily, antioxidant serum, and lifestyle sun management — the single highest-return habit in any skin quality protocol.

Gut-Skin Optimization

Microbiome balance and intestinal barrier integrity directly influence skin inflammation, barrier function, and how skin heals after procedures.

Medical-Grade Topicals

Prescription-grade retinoids, growth factors, and barrier-supportive actives that create the skin environment treatments build on — not from a spa shelf.
