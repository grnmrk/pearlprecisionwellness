Privacy
Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026  ·  Pearl Precision Wellness, Denver, Colorado

Pearl Precision Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit pearlprecisionwellness.com or engage with our services. By using this website, you agree to the practices described below.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways — information you provide directly, and information collected automatically when you visit our site.

Information you provide:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through contact or booking forms
  • Health history, symptoms, and details shared during consultations or intake
  • Communications sent to us directly via email or form submissions
  • Newsletter sign-up preferences

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5)

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Deliver and improve our functional medicine services
  • Send appointment confirmations, follow-ups, and care-related communications
  • Send educational content or wellness updates — only with your explicit consent
  • Analyze how visitors use our website and improve its performance
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

03

Health Information & HIPAA

Important: Health-related information shared with Pearl Precision Wellness in the context of a care relationship is treated as Protected Health Information (PHI) and handled in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

A separate Notice of Privacy Practices (NPP) governing the use and disclosure of your PHI will be provided at or before your first clinical interaction. That document outlines your rights as a patient and our obligations as a healthcare provider.

Information submitted through general website contact forms — before a formal care relationship is established — is treated as personal data under this Privacy Policy, not as PHI.

04

Sharing Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party platforms that help us operate our services, including our booking platform (Practice Better) and email tools. These providers are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal obligations: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Pearl Precision Wellness or others.
  • With your consent: Any other sharing occurs only with your explicit, prior consent.

We do not share health information with insurers, employers, or any third party for commercial purposes without your explicit written authorization.

05

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and understand how visitors interact with our content. We may use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — tools such as Google Analytics that collect anonymized usage data to help us improve the site

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. You may also opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

06

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy and comply with our legal obligations. Clinical records and health information are retained in accordance with Colorado state law and applicable healthcare regulations. General contact data is deleted upon request.

07

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards — including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews of our service providers — to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

08

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly
  • Data portability — request your data in a portable format where feasible

Colorado residents may have additional rights under the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@pearlprecisionwellness.com. We will respond within 45 days of a verifiable request.

09

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at hello@pearlprecisionwellness.com and we will delete it promptly.

10

Third-Party Links

This website may link to third-party platforms including Practice Better, Instagram, and LinkedIn. This Privacy Policy applies only to pearlprecisionwellness.com. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites and encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Material changes will be noted by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the website after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about your privacy? We take data protection seriously and are happy to help.
