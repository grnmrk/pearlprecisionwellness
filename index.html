Meet Your Provider

Megan Pattee, PA-C

Physician Assistant · Functional Medicine & Dermatology

Megan is a board-certified Physician Assistant with a clinical foundation in dermatology and advanced training in functional medicine. Early in her career, she developed a deep appreciation for the visible manifestations of internal health — from hair loss and acne to inflammatory and autoimmune skin conditions.

While she valued conventional treatments, she found herself wanting deeper answers. That question ultimately led her to pursue advanced training in functional medicine.

Through this lens, Megan expanded her approach beyond symptom control to systems-based investigation — examining hormonal signaling, metabolic health, gut function, inflammation, stress physiology, and nutritional status as interconnected contributors to health.

Megan believes that when internal physiology is supported appropriately, external health benefits. Her work is centered on identifying what is driving the pattern, and addressing it with intention and precision.
