How we approach care

A Model Built
Around You —
Not a Template

Most healthcare visits are designed around constraints: 15-minute appointments, insurance billing codes, and treatment algorithms that assume the same symptoms share the same cause. Functional medicine operates from a fundamentally different premise.

We take time — significant time — to understand your full history, order testing that's relevant to your specific presentation, and build protocols around what your data actually shows. Nothing here is assumed. Everything is investigated.

Our four-stage model moves from comprehensive assessment through advanced diagnostics and personalized protocol to ongoing optimization — with each stage building on the last. The process is iterative, not linear: we test, refine, and adjust as your physiology responds and your goals evolve.

Stage One

Comprehensive
Assessment

Before any test is ordered or protocol is written, we need to understand you — fully. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

01

Your first comprehensive appointment is not a 15-minute intake. It's a thorough, structured conversation covering your complete health timeline — from childhood through today. We document every symptom, every diagnosis, every medication, every treatment you've tried and how your body responded.

We explore your family health history, your stress and lifestyle patterns, your sleep, your diet, your gut function, your cycle history, your skin, your energy trajectory — the full picture. We review any prior labs or imaging you bring, reinterpreting conventional results through a functional lens.

This level of context is what allows us to order the right tests — not a blanket panel, but a targeted workup specific to what your history suggests is happening. The assessment is the most important appointment in your care.

What's covered

  • Comprehensive health history intake (60–90 minutes)
  • Symptom timeline and pattern mapping
  • Prior lab review and functional reinterpretation
  • Medication, supplement, and treatment history
  • Diet, sleep, stress, and lifestyle assessment
  • Family history and genetic context
  • Initial diagnostic hypothesis and testing plan

Stage Two

Advanced
Diagnostics

Targeted functional testing selected specifically for your presentation — going far beyond what standard labs assess or how they're typically interpreted.

02

We order what your case requires — nothing more, nothing less. Functional testing goes significantly further than a standard metabolic panel or basic thyroid screen. We assess hormones across their full cascade, evaluate gut microbiome composition and barrier integrity, map cortisol rhythm throughout the day, and examine how nutrients are being absorbed, converted, and utilized at the cellular level.

Crucially, we interpret results against optimal ranges, not just population reference ranges designed to flag disease. A value that sits within "normal" may still represent meaningful dysfunction when placed in the context of your specific symptoms, history, and physiology.

Labs are coordinated through our preferred functional testing partners. Many kits ship directly to your home. We review every result in detail with you and use findings to build your protocol.

Testing may include

  • Comprehensive hormonal panels (sex hormones, thyroid, adrenal)
  • GI-MAP stool analysis and SIBO breath testing
  • DUTCH Complete hormone metabolite testing
  • Intestinal permeability and food sensitivity panels
  • Micronutrient, genetic, and detoxification analysis
  • Cardiometabolic, inflammatory, and immune markers
  • Organic acids and mitochondrial function testing

Stage Three

Personalized
Protocol

Evidence-based, stepwise therapeutic intervention built directly from your data. No generic plans, no templates, no one-size approaches.

03

Your protocol is assembled from your assessment findings and testing results — combining the most effective evidence-based interventions for your specific physiological picture. We sequence interventions thoughtfully: foundational work first, layering more targeted therapies as the system stabilizes and responds.

Protocols are multi-modal by design, drawing on nutrition, nutraceuticals, botanical medicine, lifestyle modification, mind-body practices, gut healing frameworks, and pharmaceutical support where clinically indicated. We do not commit to a single approach before understanding what's driving the pattern.

You receive a detailed, written protocol with clear rationale for every recommendation — not a supplement list with no explanation. We want you to understand your care, not just follow it.

Protocol elements may include

  • Targeted nutraceutical and supplement recommendations
  • Therapeutic dietary protocols and elimination strategies
  • Gut restoration using the 5R framework
  • Hormonal support — natural and pharmaceutical where appropriate
  • Stress physiology and HPA axis interventions
  • Sleep and circadian rhythm optimization
  • Movement, breathwork, and mind-body prescriptions

Stage Four

Ongoing
Optimization

Health is not a fixed destination. Ongoing monitoring, retesting, and protocol refinement ensure you keep moving forward as your physiology evolves.

04

Functional medicine is not a one-appointment discipline. After your initial protocol is underway, we schedule structured follow-up appointments to assess your response — both subjectively through symptom tracking and objectively through strategic retesting of key markers.

Protocols are refined as you improve. When something isn't moving the needle as expected, we investigate why. When you reach your initial goals, we raise the bar — shifting from recovery to true optimization. Hormones change with seasons, stress, age, and life circumstances. Gut health fluctuates. Nutritional needs shift. We stay with you through that.

Long-term patients develop a deep, ongoing relationship with their own physiology — understanding what their body is telling them and having the tools to respond. That's the real outcome we're building toward.

Ongoing care includes

  • Structured follow-up appointments (30–60 minutes)
  • Strategic retesting of relevant markers
  • Protocol adjustments based on response and evolving goals
  • Seasonal and life-stage protocol adaptation
  • Coordination with other specialists where appropriate
  • Messaging support between appointments
  • Long-term health optimization and longevity strategy

Investment & Pricing

A Direct-Pay Practice — Here's Why

Pearl Precision Wellness operates as a direct-pay functional medicine practice. We do not bill insurance directly. This is a deliberate choice — not a limitation.

Insurance reimbursement models are structured around 15-minute visits, standardized billing codes, and treatments that match approved diagnoses. Functional medicine — with its comprehensive intake appointments, advanced diagnostic testing, and personalized multi-modal protocols — cannot be delivered within those constraints without compromising the quality of care.

By operating outside of insurance, we can spend the time your care requires, order the testing your case actually needs, and give you the clinical attention that produces real outcomes. Pricing is discussed at consultation and varies based on services received.

HSA / FSA Funds

Most functional medicine consultations and many testing costs qualify for reimbursement through Health Savings Accounts (HSA) and Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA). We're happy to provide documentation to support your claims.

Out-of-Network Reimbursement

Many insurance plans offer out-of-network benefits. We can provide itemized superbills for you to submit to your insurer for potential partial reimbursement. Coverage varies by plan.

Transparent Pricing

Consultation fees, testing costs, and supplement recommendations are communicated clearly upfront. We never add unexpected charges, and we'll always explain what we're recommending and why before proceeding.

Is this right for you?

Who We Work Best With

Our model is a strong fit for patients who are committed to understanding their health at a deeper level and willing to invest time and energy in meaningful change — not just a quick prescription.

  • You've been told your labs are normal but still feel unwell
  • You have chronic symptoms that conventional medicine hasn't resolved
  • You want to understand the root cause — not just manage symptoms
  • You're dealing with hormonal, gut, thyroid, skin, or energy concerns
  • You're motivated to make lifestyle and dietary changes that support healing
  • You value time with your provider and want your history actually heard
  • You're interested in long-term health optimization, not just crisis management
  • You're open to an integrative approach that combines conventional and functional medicine

Transparency

When We May Not Be the Right Fit

We're not a replacement for emergency medicine or acute care. If you're experiencing a medical emergency, go to your nearest ER or call 911.

We're also not the right fit if you're looking for a quick prescription without investigation, or if you're not ready to engage with the lifestyle and dietary work that functional medicine requires. Real, lasting change takes time and participation — and we'll always be honest with you about that.

We don't offer services covered by Medicaid or Medicare, and we don't replace your primary care physician — though we work well alongside one.
